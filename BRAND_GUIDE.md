## Brand color system

### Primary colors
Use for GLM’s main identity across digital and print.

| Name         | HEX      | RGB           | Usage                    |
|--------------|----------|---------------|--------------------------|
| **Deep Navy**   | `#0F172A` | 15, 23, 42    | Headers, primary surfaces |
| **Electric Blue** | `#2563EB` | 37, 99, 235   | CTAs, links, accents      |

### Secondary colors
Supporting palette for UI, dashboards, and text hierarchy.

| Name        | HEX      | RGB           | Usage              |
|-------------|----------|---------------|--------------------|
| **Off Black**  | `#1C1F24` | 28, 31, 36    | Body text, borders |
| **Light Grey** | `#F2F4F7` | 242, 244, 247 | Backgrounds, cards |

---

## Typography system

### Primary typeface: **Montserrat**
- **Use:** Headings, dashboard titles, navigation, primary actions.
- **Weights:** Medium (500), SemiBold (600), Bold (700).
- **Role:** Structured, modern SaaS identity.

### Secondary typeface: **Inter**
- **Use:** Body text, tables, financial data, contracts, system UI.
- **Weights:** Regular (400), Medium (500).
- **Role:** Readability and operational clarity.

## Implementation notes

- **Filament:** Primary color = Electric Blue; sidebar/header can use Deep Navy.
- **Fonts:** Load Montserrat + Inter (e.g. Google Fonts or Bunny Fonts) in the panel/layout.
- **Contrast:** Use Light Grey or white on Deep Navy / Off Black for text.
